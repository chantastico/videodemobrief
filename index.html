Hero tagline : Turn Your Creator Network Into Your Revenue Engine

Would resell Socialscale as part of their offering

Pain Points
Hero pain
No attribution — you're guessing who sold
Creators are posting every day. Sales are coming in. But the brand has no idea which creator drove which sale. Every payout is a gut call, not a data decision.
Everything is fragmented
DMs on one platform, briefs in email, tracking in spreadsheets, payments by hand. The team lives in eight tabs and still doesn't have the full picture.
One person holds everything in their head
When the creator manager leaves or is out sick, the whole program stalls. Payments, briefs, creator relationships — it all walks out with them.

Script Ideas
1
1. The Chaos
Show the fragmented reality of managing creators today. DM threads. Spreadsheets. Manual payments. The feeling of having everything everywhere and nothing working together. Fast cuts — make it feel exhausting.
Suggested VO
"You have 30 creators. Four campaigns running. And zero idea which one drove last month's revenue."
2
2. The Cost
Slow down. Let the frustration land. Someone staring at analytics that show traffic but no attribution. The quiet cost of not knowing. Then contrast — the brands winning aren't guessing.
Suggested VO
"You're paying on gut feeling. The brands ahead of you aren't."
3
3. The Product Reveal
Clean reveal of the Socialscale dashboard. Logo appears. The platform feels calm, organised, premium. Everything the chaos section wasn't. Let it breathe for a moment.
Suggested VO
"Socialscale. One system to manage your creators, run your campaigns, and own your results."
4
4. Campaign Management
Campaign dashboard — creator list, brief statuses, content approvals. Someone approves a post in one click. The contrast with email chains is implicit — don't say it, show it.
Suggested VO
"Brief creators, approve content, track every post — without leaving one place."
5
5. Storefront Affiliates
A creator storefront — branded, clean, products listed. Cut to the revenue view: which storefront sold, how much. One number. Make it feel like a revelation — this is what attribution looks like.
Suggested VO
"Give every creator their own branded storefront — free. See who's selling. See what converts. Pay only for what performs."
6
6. Creator Hub
Creator Hub — mobile screen. A creator checking their stats, uploading content, seeing their earnings. Then brand-side: all creators in one place. No DMs. No chasing. Your program, your system.
Suggested VO
"Your creators. Your hub. Your domain. Everything in one place — and your program keeps running even when your team doesn't."
7
7. Social Intelligence Teaser
A glimpse — blurred or in motion — of the intelligence dashboard. AI recommendations, attribution graphs. Not fully revealed. Something powerful is coming. Create a sense of what's possible.
Suggested VO
"And coming mid-2026 — the intelligence layer that tells you exactly where to act. Before your competitors figure it out."
8
8. CTA
Clean. Confident. Socialscale logo. URL. One button. The tagline lands last — it's the thing they leave with.
Suggested VO
"Run your creator program like you own it. Get early access — free to start. socialscale.ai"
